Bangladeshi Chicken Korma

This rich and flavorful chicken korma is a traditional Bangladeshi dish often served at special occasions. The creamy sauce is made with yogurt, spices, and ground nuts for a deliciously fragrant curry.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups yogurt
  • 2 onions, finely sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 cup cashew nuts
  • 1/2 cup oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Grind garlic, ginger, and cashew nuts into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry onions until golden brown.
  3. Add chicken pieces and brown on all sides.
  4. Add all spices and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add yogurt and nut paste, mix well.
  6. Cover and simmer for 25-30 minutes until chicken is tender.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with naan or rice.
